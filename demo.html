How does it work?

  1. The extension automatically scans all text on the page
  2. URLs are detected by a robust pattern
  3. Matches are converted into clickable links
  4. Links open in a new tab for safety
  5. Dynamic content is processed as it appears

Supported formats:

  • ✅ https://example.com
  • ✅ http://example.com
  • ✅ ftp://example.com
  • ✅ www.example.com
  • ✅ URLs with parameters and paths
